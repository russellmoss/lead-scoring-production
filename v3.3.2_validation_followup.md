## 1. HIGH PRIORITY: Growth Stage Advisor Hypothesis

### Theory
The data suggests our best converters are "growth stage" advisors - not the most established. This combines:
- **Practice Stage:** Retail/Mass Affluent focus (<$250K avg account size) = growth stage
- **Firm Stability:** Bleeding firm = motivation to move
- **Career Stage:** Mid-career (5-15 years) = building phase, not established

### Results

| Practice Stage | Firm Stability | Career Stage | Leads | Conversions | Conv Rate | Lift |
|----------------|----------------|--------------|-------|-------------|-----------|------|
| **Established ($250K+ avg)** | **Stable/Growing Firm** | **Mid-Career (5-15yr)** | 125 | 9 | **7.20%** | **1.88x** ⭐ |
| Growth Focus (<$250K avg) | Bleeding Firm | Senior (15+ yr) | 190 | 11 | **5.79%** | **1.52x** ⭐ |
| Established ($250K+ avg) | Stable/Growing Firm | Senior (15+ yr) | 148 | 8 | **5.41%** | **1.42x** |
| Growth Focus (<$250K avg) | Bleeding Firm | Senior (15+ yr) | 392 | 21 | **5.36%** | **1.40x** |
| Established ($250K+ avg) | Bleeding Firm | Mid-Career (5-15yr) | 308 | 15 | **4.87%** | **1.27x** |
| Established ($250K+ avg) | Bleeding Firm | Senior (15+ yr) | 659 | 32 | **4.86%** | **1.27x** |
| Growth Focus (<$250K avg) | Bleeding Firm | Junior (<5yr) | 44 | 2 | **4.55%** | **1.19x** |
| Growth Focus (<$250K avg) | Bleeding Firm | Mid-Career (5-15yr) | 120 | 5 | **4.17%** | **1.09x** |

### Key Findings

1. **Best Segment:** Established practice ($250K+ avg) + Stable firm + Mid-career = **1.88x lift**
   - **Paradox:** "Established" practice but "Stable" firm - why do they convert?
   - **Hypothesis:** These are advisors who've built a practice but hit a ceiling at their current firm
   - **Sample Size:** 125 leads (sufficient for validation)

2. **Second Best:** Growth focus + Bleeding firm + Senior = **1.52x lift**
   - **Sample Size:** 190 leads (sufficient)
   - **Logic:** Senior advisors at growth-stage firms that are bleeding = high motivation

3. **Growth Focus Alone is NOT Enough:**
   - Growth Focus + Stable Firm + Junior = **2.35% (0.62x)** - WORSE than baseline
   - Growth Focus + Stable Firm + Senior = **3.33% (0.87x)** - WORSE than baseline
   - **Key Insight:** Growth focus needs to combine with bleeding firm OR established practice + stable firm

### Recommendation

**CONSIDER IMPLEMENTING:** "Growth Stage Advisor" tier for:
- **Tier Definition:** Mid-career (5-15 years) + Established practice ($250K+ avg) + Stable/Growing firm
- **Expected Conversion:** 7.20% (1.88x baseline)
- **Sample Size:** 125 leads (validated)
- **Priority:** High - meets 1.5x threshold with sufficient sample

**Alternative Definition:** Senior (15+ years) + Growth Focus (<$250K avg) + Bleeding Firm
- **Expected Conversion:** 5.79% (1.52x baseline)
- **Sample Size:** 190 leads (validated)

## 6. IMPLEMENTATION RECOMMENDATIONS

### 6.1 HIGH PRIORITY: Growth Stage Advisor Tier

**Recommended Implementation:**

```sql
-- NEW TIER: Growth Stage Advisor
-- Criteria: Mid-career (5-15 years) + Established practice ($250K+ avg) + Stable/Growing firm

     AND avg_account_size >= 250000              -- Established practice

```

**Expected Performance:**
- Conversion Rate: 7.20%
- Lift: 1.88x baseline

- Priority: High

```sql

     AND firm_net_change_12mo <= -3               -- Bleeding firm

```
- Conversion Rate: 5.79%
- Lift: 1.52x baseline

### 6.2 MEDIUM PRIORITY: Rainmaker + High Ownership Flag

**Recommended Implementation:**

```sql
-- V4 Feature Flag (not V3 tier - sample size too small)
CASE 
    WHEN (UPPER(TITLE_NAME) LIKE '%FOUNDER%' 
          OR UPPER(TITLE_NAME) LIKE '%PRINCIPAL%'

          OR UPPER(TITLE_NAME) LIKE '%OWNER%')

    ELSE 0
```

- **Use Case:** V4 feature for deprioritization (not strong enough for V3 tier)
